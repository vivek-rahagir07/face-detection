1. What is CognitoAttend?
CognitoAttend is a smart digital attendance management system designed to track presence accurately using QR-based check-ins, real-time monitoring, and automated reports.

2. How does QR attendance work?
An admin generates a time-limited QR code. Users scan it using their device, enter basic details, and their attendance is marked instantly. The QR refreshes automatically to prevent misuse.

3. Can students or users fake attendance?
No. CognitoAttend uses dynamic QR refresh, session validation, and optional location/device checks to minimize proxy or duplicate attendance.

4. Is registration mandatory for marking attendance?
Not always. CognitoAttend supports both:
- Registered users (with Reg No / Course)
- Unregistered or visitor entries (e.g., library, events)

5. Can attendance be edited later?
Yes. Authorized admins can edit user details, correct entries, or delete records with proper access control.

6. Does CognitoAttend generate reports?
Absolutely. You can generate:
- Daily / date-wise attendance
- Master reports
- CSV and PDF exports
These are useful for audits, records, and compliance.

7. Is CognitoAttend suitable for colleges only?
No. It can be used for:
- Colleges & schools
- Libraries
- Corporates & offices
- Events & workshops
- Training institutes

8. Can I track attendance history of an individual?
Yes. Each user has a detailed attendance history, showing dates, time, and presence status.

9. Does it work on mobile and desktop?
Yes. CognitoAttend is device-friendly and works smoothly on mobiles, tablets, laptops, and large screens.

10. Is internet required?
Yes. An active internet connection is required for real-time sync, QR refresh, and report generation.

11. How secure is the data?
CognitoAttend follows secure data handling practices, role-based access, and controlled visibility to ensure privacy and integrity.
